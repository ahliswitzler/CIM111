Biography

Ahli is a Jersey-born songwriter with a passion for Indie-Rock, Folk, and all things R&B. With influences that span from Thundercat to Joni Mitchell, their music is as original as it is catchy. They have been songwriting for about twelve years and have played at some of the top venues around Miami (from Churchills to Lagniappe), New York City (Rockwood Music Hall), and Nashville (The Listening Room).Keep a look out for their upcoming single "HONEY" and their featured performance at Musikfest in August 2019.
