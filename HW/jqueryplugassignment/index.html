Family Vacation

My mom normally dictates where we go on our vacations. Since I was born, I have gotten the opportunity to go to most of Europe, a little bit of the middle East, and almost everywhere with moss or ice due to my mom's artistic pursuits in realism paintings (she basically loves trees and the cold in her work). Other than that my family has also found itself everywhere that my extended family has ended up; frequently visiting the Warm Springs Indian Reservation in Oregon, or down in Texas with my grandparents.
